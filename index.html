Scenario 1

Initial Facts

  • Saturday afternoon, a certain group of hackers defaced all Joomla made websites hosted in GWHS.
  • All user accounts, including admin accounts has been exposed and dumped on the Internet.

Inject 1

  • The passwords in the exposed data are displayed in plain text.
  • The hackers also exposed that the agency is using an insecure password hashing algorithm, they didn't reveal what kind.

Scenario 2

Initial Facts

  • After the successful implementation of the Philippine ID System.
  • Months later, PSA and DICT received an email from an anonymous person telling that the database of the said system has been compromised.

Inject 1

  • The claim is valid and the data have already been dumped on the Internet.

Questions

  1. Who is in charge of the investigation?
  2. Who is part of the Incident Response Team?
  3. Do we need outside support/help? Why or Why not
  4. How do we prevent it from recurring.
